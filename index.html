Welcome to Metropolis Coffee, the best the city has to offer.

Here at Metropolis Coffee, we want to provide you with the most satisfactory coffee experience you can get. For years our dream has been to bring amazing atmosphere, service, and coffee to Raskland. Back in 2016 when we started, we looked around our home town and felt that there was a true lack of coffee presence and culture, and that we were uniquely gifted to do something about the void. Thus, Metropolis Coffee was born.

Ever felt frustrated trying to find a coffee company that consistently offers high quality coffee? At Metropolis, we carefully select only the best coffees from all around the world. When you pick up a bag of our coffee, you can be confident you are getting the best that your money can buy. We roast and package fresh in our cafe, right here in Raskland, MO.
